About Us

Who Are We?

In a world where people seek flexibility and ease, businesses are heading to cope with the sharing-economy, depending more on human reliance and social networks. Amtea is a crowd-shipping platform that transports small and medium-sized parcels within travelers’ unused luggage space, where we connect a fellow traveler with someone who wants to send items to the same traveler’s destination. Amtea was initiated and developed by four Industrial Engineering students from the Jordan University of Science and Technology when they participated in the world’s largest business and entrepreneurship competition among university students ( Hult Prize Foundation ), which opened for them a lot of doors and achievements in the business world and led them to establish their own, amtea.

Our Mission

Amtea will escalate and support the shipping industry, With this as our primary mission, we will make travelling and item shipments more attainable for everyone at any one time. By boosting social responsibilities to help the world achieve what they need with minimal efforts and highest quality services. We will work closely with our valued customers and travelers to establish solid business relationships within the local and national markets as well. Todays’ customer is seeking the most cost-effective alternatives while expecting the best possible services from its vendor base. amtea Co. mission will be to provide all of the above to the Shipping, Crowd-sourcing, and Logistics marketplace.

Our Vision

Our world is developing faster than ever and everyone needs to be able to cope with the time being. We aim to introduce crowd-shipping to our cultures to open new horizons to our industries. For now is the best time to be alive since this escalation has offered opportunities that we must acquire without hesitation. In Amtea, our vision is to spread and establish the concept of the sharing economy in our local and national societies, so it is our chance to benefit from it by believing in one another to grow and develop. People need to be able to depend on each other and unite, this is the image that we draw for our future.
